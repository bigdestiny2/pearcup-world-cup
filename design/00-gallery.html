PearCup · Design exploration

Three anime directions
for the World Cup app.

Each spec is a fully self-contained, open-in-browser HTML file you can click into. They all share the same PearCup structure — onboarding, home hub, bracket, watch party, minigame — re-skinned into a distinct anime aesthetic with 3D buttons, motion, and little character avatars.

Spec 01 · Dark cyberpunk

Neo-Tokyo Strikers

Midnight neon, holographic pitch lines, magenta/cyan duotone. Glow-rimmed 3D buttons that press in like arcade keys. Avatars are sharp-eyed idol-mecha hybrids.

Glow buttons Holo pitch Duotone Spec 02 · Manga action

Shonen Pitch

Bold ink outlines, halftone speed lines, blazing yellow + red energy. Chunky candy 3D buttons with a satisfying bounce. Avatars are spiky-haired strikers with fierce grins.

Speed lines Chunky 3D Ink outline Spec 03 · Pastel magical

Sakura Stadium

Soft pinks, twilight lavender, glassy 3D pills with inner glow. Floating petals and gentle parallax. Avatars are chibi mascots with starry eyes and round cheeks.
